Support & User Manual - MyClassBell

Introduction

Welcome to MyClassBell, the powerful offline scheduling tool designed for educators and students. Our app helps you create, manage, and customize your daily schedules with ease. This support page provides detailed instructions on using MyClassBell, along with answers to frequently asked questions.

Getting Started

After downloading and installing MyClassBell, simply launch the app. The main screen displays today's schedule along with real‑time progress indicators. All data is stored locally, so you can work offline without any hassle.

Creating and Managing Schedules

Creating a Schedule

To create a schedule, tap the “Add Schedule” button in the settings screen. You can create unique schedules for each day or use the same schedule throughout the week. Enter your class periods, start times, end times, and break intervals using the intuitive data entry interface.

Editing and Renaming Periods

You can easily update your schedule by tapping on any period in the schedule table. The editor allows you to rename periods, adjust start/end times, and modify break durations. All changes are saved locally and update immediately.

Assigning Schedules to Days

Use the notebook‑style day tabs at the top of the app to select a day. In the settings menu, map each day to a specific schedule. If no schedule is assigned, a default gray appearance is used.

Customizing Colors & Themes

MyClassBell offers a variety of color customization features. You can change the colors for the header, table, and individual day tabs. This allows you to personalize the look of your schedule, making it easier to differentiate between days or reflect your school’s branding.

Real-Time Features & Time Management

The app provides a dynamic progress bar and countdown timer that display the remaining time in the current class or break period. These real‑time features help you stay on track throughout your day. Notifications alert you when a class or break is about to end, so you never miss an important transition.

Offline Functionality

MyClassBell works entirely offline. All your schedule data, settings, and customizations are stored locally on your device, ensuring that you have access to your schedule anytime—even without an internet connection.

User Manual

Navigation

The main screen consists of:

  • A top header displaying the current date and time, along with a settings button.
  • A progress bar and countdown circle indicating the remaining time in the current period.
  • Notebook‑style day tabs that let you switch between days.
  • A full‑screen schedule table that lists your periods, start times, and end times.

Using the Schedule Table

The schedule table updates automatically when you assign a new schedule or modify an existing one. Simply scroll through the table to view all periods for the selected day. Text in the table can be selected and copied for easy reference.

Settings & Customization

In the settings menu, you can:

  • Assign different schedules for each day or use one schedule for all days.
  • Rename periods and adjust class times with a simple tap-to-edit interface.
  • Customize color themes for the header, table, and day tabs to suit your personal style.

Advanced Time Management

MyClassBell’s real-time progress features and countdown timers help you manage your day effectively. Use these tools to ensure you transition smoothly from one period to the next, and take advantage of the “Go to Today” popup to quickly return to the current day’s schedule.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q: Can I change my schedule after setting it up?

A: Yes, you can easily edit and update your schedule at any time by tapping on the period you wish to change.

Q: How do I assign different schedules for each day?

A: In the settings menu, you can map each day to a specific schedule. If a day has no assigned schedule, it will display in a default gray color.

Q: Does MyClassBell work offline?

A: Absolutely. All data is stored locally on your device, so you can access your schedule even without an internet connection.

Q: How can I customize the colors and themes?

A: In the settings screen, you can select custom colors for various parts of the app, including the header, table, and day tabs, so your schedule reflects your personal style.
